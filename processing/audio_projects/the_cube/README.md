<p align="center">
An auditory experience born out of a desire to work visually with audio in Processing. An idea became a sketch, became code, and along the way various features were added resulting in a fun and engaging user experience.
</p>

## Instructions

<p>- click and drag the center vertex of the cube to activiate different sound spaces. 
<br><br>- KEY 'T': launch the tutorial mode. In tutorial mode navigate each step with the RIGHT ARROW KEY. Press KEY ‘R’ to exit and reset The Cube. All other functionality will be disabled in tutorial mode. 
<br><br>- KEY 'R': reset the cube to its origin point.
<br><br>- KEY 'W': show/hide The Cube wire frame view. Pressing SHIFT + ‘W’ will cycle through the available wire frame colors. 
<br><br>- KEY 'P': show/hide the the pulse (audio) analyzer cube. Pressing SHIFT + ‘P’ will cycle through the available colors for the pulse analyzer. 
<br><br>- SPACE KEY: hide/show the audio playback status bar
<br><br>- NUM KEY: pressing the NUM KEYS (1-9) mutes/unmutes the corresponding audio for the specified zone number. To mute zones 10, 11, and 12 use KEYS ‘0’, ‘-’, and ‘ =’ respectively.
<br><br>- CTRL + NUM KEYS: pressing the CTRL KEY plus any NUM KEY (1-9)will show/hide a visual representation of the reach of the corresponding sound space along with the epicenter. To highlight zones 10, 11, and 12 use KEYS ‘0’, ‘-’, and ‘ =’ respectively.
<br><br>- LEFT ARROW: mute sounds spaces 1-4
<br><br>- UP ARROW: mute sound spaces 5-8
<br><br>- RIGHT ARROW: mute sound spaces 9-12
<br><br>- DOWN KEY: pressing the down key will hide all of the sound spaces that are set to show.
<br><br>- SHIFT + NUM KEY: pressing the SHIFT KEY plus any NUM KEY (1-9) solos/unsolos the corresponding audio for the specified zone number. To solo zones 10, 11, and 12 use KEYS ‘0’, ‘-’, and ‘ =’ respectively.
</p>
